Helpful Tips

Increase Your Chances

Simple tips to help recover your belongings faster.

📸

Add a Clear Photo

Listings with photos get 3x more responses. Upload front and back if possible.

📍

Be Specific on Location

Mention exact spot like "Library 2nd floor near window" not just "Library".

⚡

Report Immediately

Post within hours of losing. The sooner it's live, the better the recovery chance.

About ProjectLOST & FOUND PORTAL

The Lost & Found Portal is a digital initiative by the Dept. of CSE at SRK Institute of Technology, Vijayawada. Students and staff can report and recover misplaced belongings securely using their Indian phone numbers.

All posts include public contact details and auto-expire after 30 days to stay fresh and relevant for the campus community.
