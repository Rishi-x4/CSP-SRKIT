# 🔍 Lost & Found — Campus Item Recovery Portal

> A secure digital platform for students and staff of SRK Institute of Technology, Vijayawada to report and reclaim lost items on campus.

---

## 📌 About the Project

The **Lost & Found Portal** is a digital initiative by the Department of CSE at SRK Institute of Technology. Students and staff can report lost or found items, browse listings, and connect with each other to return belongings — all within a secure, campus-only environment using Indian phone numbers.

## ✨ Features

- 🔐 **Phone-based Login** — Indian numbers only (+91), OTP verified
- 📋 **Report Lost or Found Items** — photo, category, location, description
- 📞 **Public Contact Details** — Indian phone number visible on every post
- 🗓️ **Auto 30-Day Expiry** — posts removed automatically after 30 days
- 💾 **Data Persistence** — posts saved locally, survive page refresh
- 🔎 **Search & Filter** — by category, location, Lost/Found type
- 📬 **Smart Claim System** — submit proof of ownership to claim an item
- ⚙️ **Admin Panel** — manage all posts and claims
- 📱 **Fully Responsive** — works on mobile and desktop
